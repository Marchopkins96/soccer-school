About us

What?


At Soccer School we provide a fun football training camp for children aged between 6 & 13. We have a qualified coaching team who oversee all the daily activities and ensure every child is getting the best experience possible.

Why?


Not only do we aim to improve childrens footballing skills, we also focus on promoting self-confidence, team building and inclusivity. We believe these are core life skills to assist a childs development.

Where and when?


We are based at York College's state of the art sports facility providing indoor and outdoor football pitches. The football camps run throughout all school half-term holidays and summer holidays. please see the bottom of the page for the address.
